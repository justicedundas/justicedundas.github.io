Welcome to Ardoch Lake

Beautiful Property

Over 5,000 feet of pristine, untouched, level waterfront and 270 acres of wilderness on Ardoch Lake in North Frontenac, the heart of Eastern Ontario's cottage country and in the Canadian Shield.

Gorgeous Lake

Firm bottomed with excellent depths of over 50 feet, sandy access into crystal clear spring-fed water. Enjoy boating, fishing, hiking, camping, and numerous winter activities in unsurpassed, private, natural beauty.

Accessible Yet Private

Access to the site by a main private road with extensive logging trails to a mixed forest of hardwoods and softwoods. Additional access to Fawn, Twin Island and Crotch Lakes which are fully surrounded by Crown Lands.

Property

Draft Plan Approved

Condominium Draft Plan Approved for 23 Residential Estate Lots (15 waterfront and 8 inland lots). Each lot is a minimum of two acres; waterfront lots have shoreline frontages ranging from 250 to 950 feet. Includes an existing two bedroom waterfront cottage (Lot 11).

Room for Activities

Ardoch Lake connects to Malcolm Lake and has a shoreline perimeter of approximately 5.8 km; lake length of 1.9 km and width of lkm; maximum depth of 56 feet with the following fish species: Walleye; Northern Pike; Smallmouth Bass; White Sucker; Rock Bass and Pumpkinseed.

Great Location

Ardoch Lake is located 35 minutes north of the village of Shar bot Lake; 1.25 hours north of Kingston; 1.5 hours west of Ottawa; in the North Frontenac Township in the County of Frontenac
